Constituency

A geographic area whose voters elect one representative to parliament or assembly.

EVM

Electronic Voting Machine — the tamper-proof device used to cast and store votes digitally.

VVPAT

Voter Verified Paper Audit Trail — prints a paper slip so you can confirm your vote was recorded correctly.

Lok Sabha

Lower house of India's parliament. 543 seats elected directly by citizens every 5 years.

Rajya Sabha

Upper house. Members elected by state legislators, not directly by citizens.

Model Code of Conduct

Rules that govern parties and candidates from announcement day until results are declared.

Affidavit

A legal declaration candidates must file disclosing criminal records, assets and liabilities.

Exit Poll

A survey of voters leaving booths, used to predict results before official counting begins.

Manifesto

A political party's official document listing their promises and plans if elected to power.

Voter ID (EPIC)

Electoral Photo Identity Card — issued by ECI, used to verify identity at polling booths.
